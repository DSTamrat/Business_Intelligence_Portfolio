# Monthly Credit Union Performance Summary

## Reporting Period
January 2024  
Data Source: fact_transactions_clean (ETL output)

---

## Executive Summary

Member activity remained strong this month, with consistent transaction volume across checking and credit card accounts. Digital channels (POS and Online) continue to dominate usage, reflecting a shift toward cashless and remote banking behavior. Branch performance varies by location, with Maryland branches showing the highest engagement.

Overall, the credit union demonstrates stable financial activity and opportunities for targeted product growth.

---

## Key Metrics

- **Total Transaction Amount:** $1,160.75  
- **Total Number of Transactions:** 6  
- **Average Transaction Amount:** $193.46  
- **Most Active Product:** Checking  
- **Top Channel:** POS (Point of Sale)

---

## Product Mix Insights

- **Checking accounts** show the highest activity.
- **Credit cards** contribute significant spend through online channels.
- **Savings and loans** show minimal activity this month.

**Opportunity:**  
Promote savings and loan products to high-engagement checking customers.

---

## Customer Insights

Top customer segments:

- Members aged 30–45 show the highest transaction frequency.
- Maryland residents (Baltimore, Bethesda, Silver Spring) dominate activity.
- Customers with multiple products (checking + credit card) generate the most revenue.

---

## Channel Usage

- **POS:** Most frequently used channel  
- **Online:** Strong for credit card transactions  
- **ATM:** Minimal usage  
- **Branch:** Used primarily for deposits

**Insight:**  
Digital adoption is strong — consider expanding online services and mobile features.

---

## Recommendations

1. **Target high-value customers** with personalized credit card offers.  
2. **Increase digital engagement** through mobile app promotions.  
3. **Promote savings and loan products** to checking-heavy customers.  
4. **Support underperforming branches** with localized marketing.  
5. **Monitor credit card utilization** to identify risk and growth opportunities.
